About

Rehab Engineering Inc was founded by Jose E. Hernandez, a registered professional engineer and his wife, a registered occupational therapist. Mr. Hernandez has degrees in Electrical Engineering and Bio-Medical Engineering and has worked hand-in-hand with the disabled population since 1973.

We have designed and installed a variety of adaptive aids ranging from communications and environmental control with just an eye blink to worksite improvements using speech recognition on a laptop. We provide comprehensive, turn-key training programs. In addition, we have designed scores of ramps and platforms in compliance to ADA standards for the Texas Rehab Commission, the Veterans Administration and school districts. We perform site surveys and provide BEFORE and AFTER illustrations on our drawings.
